## Notes

- SSE responses are streamed chunk-by-chunk from upstream to client
- OAGW does not buffer the entire response; it forwards chunks as they arrive
- Rate limit headers are sent with the initial response headers
- Connection remains open until upstream sends `[DONE]` or closes
