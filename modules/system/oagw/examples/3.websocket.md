## Notes

- WebSocket connections are upgraded at the OAGW proxy layer
- OAGW maintains two connections: client ↔ OAGW ↔ upstream
- Messages are forwarded bidirectionally without modification (unless plugins transform them)
- Rate limiting applies to the initial connection, not individual messages
- Connection idle timeout applies; configure via upstream settings
- Auth token is injected during the upgrade handshake, not in WebSocket frames
